selecting distinct records:-

    select distinct department from products
Gets us all the unique values of department.

To get count of how many unqiue values are there in a column:-
    select count(DISTINCT department) FROM products

Similar to group by. we can use group by in place of distinct but not distinct in group by.

Get the unique values of department and name:-
    select DISTINCT department, name FROM products
here, the department could occur twice or more but not with the same name. baiscally a unique combination of the two.

Q. Get the number of unique manufacturers from phones
    select count(DISTINCT manufacturer) FROM phones
